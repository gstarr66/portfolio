About Me

Hello, I'm Gary! For over three decades, I've worked across virtually every level of Information Technology. Over my career, I've spent **12 years as an IT Director** and **4 years as an IT Manager**, leading cross-functional teams, executing organizational-scale infrastructure upgrades, and managing strict multi-million-dollar budgets.

But while my background is in executive IT management, I've never lost my love for raw, hands-on software creation and system engineering. I'm a builder at heart. Whether it's crafting high-performance scripts to stabilize complex websites, rolling out organizational database structures, or developing fully customized AI-based platforms, I love diving deep into the code to build things that actually work.
