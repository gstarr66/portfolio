Metric Definitions
vel_6moUnits sold/month over 6 months — normal sales only (excludes short-dated & rep-discount). Primary reorder metric.
vel_3moUnits sold/month over 3 months (all sales). Shows recent trend direction.
vel_12moUnits sold/month over 12 months (all sales). Used for bucket scoring.
MOSMonths of Stock — current inventory ÷ vel_6mo. How long stock will last at current pace.
At Risk UnitsUnits that won't sell before expiration — FIFO lot-by-lot calculation using vel_6mo.
Critical UnitsUnits in lots expiring within 30 days.
Trend %% change: recent 3mo vs prior 3mo (normal sales only). >5% = up, <−5% = down.
GP/moGross profit per month (annual gross profit ÷ 12).
COGSWeighted average cost from purchase orders.
Margin %Actual margin: (Revenue − COGS) ÷ Revenue over 12 months.
